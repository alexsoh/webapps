Epoxy Tooling System

Gel coat, laminating, casting and modelling compounds engineered for patterns, moulds and tooling—from machinable surfaces to heat-resistant systems, backed by Miracon since 1984.

Overview

Multipurpose epoxy for tooling applications

Miracon Epoxy Tooling systems provide a complete list of multipurpose epoxy compounds for different applications. There are gel coat systems, laminating systems, casting/backing systems, and putties/modeling paste. Every epoxy system has a selection of various product types which can be matched to the specific requirements of the tooling industry.
