Who We Are

Best Formulating Epoxy Systems Manufacturer in Malaysia Since 1984

Established in 1984, Miracon initially focused on formulating and manufacturing epoxy systems, primarily serving the construction industry. Notably, we played a significant role in supplying materials for the construction of Southeast Asia's longest bridge, the Penang Bridge, collaborating with Hyundai Engineering & Construction Company.
